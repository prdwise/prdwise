# Writing PRD Sections

Include these sections in PRDs for writing/content projects.

## Target Audience
[Primary readers and their characteristics]
- Who are they?
- What do they already know?
- What do they care about?
- Reading level/expertise

## Publication Channels
[Where this will be published]
- Primary: [blog, newsletter, external publication]
- Secondary: [syndication, repurposing]

## Content Format
[Type and structure]
- Format: [article, guide, tutorial, case study, interview]
- Word count target: [range]
- Structure: [sections, narrative flow]

## SEO/Discovery
[How readers will find this]
- Target keywords: [primary, secondary]
- Search intent: [informational, transactional, navigational]
- Related content to link: [internal, external]
